About

This is my microblog where I write down (hopefully correct) facts and links to more elaborate articles. I’m keeping the individual blog posts concise because

  1. it’s a format I haven’t encountered often on the internet
  2. it better suits me to look up facts in the future
  3. if I keep blog posts short then I’m more likely to continue this microblog ;) .

Feedback on false statements, better ways to do stuff or general feedback is always welcome via email.
